Simple Vegetable Stirfry



Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil
  • Red bell peppers
  • Yellow bell peppers
  • Sugar snap peas
  • Carrots
  • Mushrooms
  • Broccoli
  • Baby corn
  • Water chestnuts
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic cloves (minced)
  • Brown sugar
  • Sesame oil
  • Chicken broth
  • Cornstarch
  • Green onions
  • Sesame seeds

How to Make:

  1. Begin by slicing bell peppers, carrots, and mushrooms into desirable portions. Afterwards place 1 Tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat and proceed by adding your bell pepper, peas, carrots, mushrooms, broccoli, baby corn, and water chestnuts. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are almost tender.

  2. Next, in a small bowl; whisk together soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar, sesame oil, chicken broth, and cornstarch.Then pour the mixture over the vegitables and cook untill sauce has thickened.

  3. Lastly, garnish by adding as much chopped green onions and sesame seeds as you please.
